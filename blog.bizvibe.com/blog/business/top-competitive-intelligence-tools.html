2.    Crunchbase

 

Crunchbase ranks 2nd on this list of the top competitive intelligence platforms. Focused on the technology sector, Crunchbase is a platform for finding business information about private and public companies. Crunchbase is best used to get financial information such as stock trends, investments, and acquisitions. Crunchbase’s data comes from four sources: their venture program, machine learning, an in-house data team, and the Crunchbase community. Crunchbase was founded in San Francisco, California as a place to track startups mentioned in TechCrunch articles.

 

3.    Semrush

 

Semrush ranks 3rd on this list of the top 10 competitor intelligence tools. Semrush is a leading competitive intelligence platform that is often used for keyword research and online ranking data, but also for tracking the online performance of a company’s competitors. Through Semrush’s, competitor research tools, you can find similar companies to yours using similar organic rankings for keywords and content.

 

4.    Talkwalker Alerts

 

An alternative to Google alerts, Talkwalker Alerts ranks 4th on this list of the top free online competitor analysis tools. Talkwalker Alerts helps users track brand names, competitors, events, or any other topic across the web. Talkwalker Alerts monitors every single mention of your brand and keywords across the internet – including news platforms, blogs, forums, websites, and even Twitter. Talkwalker Alerts help users also figure out what customers are saying about you and the competition?

 

5.    Ahrefs

 

Ahrefs ranks 5th on the list of the best competitive intelligence platforms of 2022. Ahrefs is a software company that develops online SEO tools and free educational materials for marketing professionals. Ahrefs is mainly used to analyze a website’s link profile, keyword rankings, and SEO health, this helps users find companies that are similar based on their online presence.

 

6.    Owletter

 

Owletter ranks 6th on this list of the top 10 competitive intelligence tools in the world. Owletter captures, stores & analyzes your competitors’ emails. Owletter automatically captures all emails sent from a website to their mailing list. It then takes a screenshot, stores it, and analyses it, alerting users about the email if it’s important.

 

7.    Wappalyzer

 

Wappalyzer ranks 7th on this list of the top 10 competitive intelligence tools in the world. Wappalyzer is a technology profiler and lead-data provider. Wappalyzer tracks over thousand web technologies across millions of websites. Wappalyzer helps companies find out the technology stack of any website.

 

8.    SimilarWeb

 

Similarweb ranks 7th on this list of the top competitive intelligence tools in the world in 2022. Similarweb is a digital intelligence provider for enterprise and small to mid-sized business customers. Similarweb provides web analytics services and offers its users information on their clients’ and competitors’ web traffic and performance.

 

9.    Hootsuite

 

Hootsuite ranks 9th on this list of the best market intelligence software companies in the world. Hootsuite Streams is a powerful tool that allows you to track keywords, competitors, and hashtags across every social network

 

10.  SpyFu

 

Rounding out this list of the top 10 competitive intelligence tools in the world is SpyFu. SpyFu is used as a competitive analysis tool to get unique insights into a competitor’s organic and PPC (pay-per-click) strategy.
