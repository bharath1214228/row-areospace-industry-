Global Manufacturing Industry Statistics

  • The United States has 12.85 million manufacturing jobs —employing 8.5% of the workforce.
  • Manufacturers contribute over $2.3 trillion to the economy of the United States every quarter.
  • China leads the world in terms of manufacturing output, with over $2.01 trillion in output.
  • Poland has the highest percentage of its workforce employed in manufacturing, followed by Germany, Italy, Turkey, and South Korea.
  • Manufacturing constitutes 27 percent of China’s overall national output, which accounts for 20 percent of the world’s manufacturing output.

1.    Volkswagen Group

 

Ranking number 1 on the list of the top 10 largest manufacturing companies in the world is the renowned German multinational automotive manufacturing company Volkswagen Group, Founded in 1937 in Berlin, Germany, with its current headquarters in Wolfsburg, Germany. The company is the largest manufacturing company in the world because it operates 100 production facilities across 27 countries. The Volkswagen Group brings in annual revenues of USD 282.9 billion,

The company’s products offerings include automobiles, commercial vehicles, internal combustion engines, motorcycles, and turbomachinery.

 

  • Founded: 1937
  • Volkswagen Group Headquarters: Wolfsburg, Germany
  • Volkswagen Group Revenue: USD 282.9 billion
  • Volkswagen Group Number of Employees: 304,174

 

2.    Toyota Group

 

The brand power of Toyota Group has always been super strong and that’s why the company is the 2nd biggest manufacturing company in the world. Toyota Group is a group of companies that have supplier, vendor and investment relationships with Toyota Industries and Toyota Motor vehicle manufacturing facilities. Toyota Group headquarters are in Toyota, Aichi, Japan and currently brings in annual revenues of USD 265.1 billion — making the company 2nd on the list of the largest manufacturing companies in the world.

 

  • Founded: 1937
  • Toyota Group Headquarters: Toyota, Aichi, Japan
  • Toyota Group Revenue: USD 265.1 billion
  • Toyota Group Number of Employees: 370,870

 

3.    Apple

 

Apple is a multinational technology company that is renowned in the world for its brand power. Aside from a very positive public perception, Apple is also a company that brings in some of the highest revenues and profits in the word. Apple’s annual revenue in 2019 was USD 260.2 billion — 3rd on the list of the largest manufacturing companies in the world.  Apple was founded In 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, and currently operates 500+ retail stores worldwide.

 

  • Founded: 1976
  • Apple Headquarters: Cupertino, California, United States
  • Apple Revenue: USD 260.2 billion
  • Apple Number of Employees: 137,000

 

4.    Samsung Electronics

 

Samsung Electronics is a South Korean multinational electronics company headquartered in Suwon, South Korea and 4th on BizVibe’s list of the top manufacturing companies in the world. The company is known for its consumer electronics and electronic components including semiconductors, batteries, chips, flash memory, and more that it manufactures for major clients such as Sony, Apple, HTC and more. Samsung was founded in 1969 and brings in annual revenues of USD 221.6 billion.

 

  • Founded: 1969
  • Samsung ElectronicsHeadquarters: Suwon-si, South Korea
  • Samsung ElectronicsRevenue: USD 221.6 billion

 

5.    Daimler Group 

 

Daimler AG is a German multinational automotive corporation, headquartered in Mercedesstraße, Stuttgart, Germany that specializes in manufacturing automotive products and parts. The company was founded in 1926 and brings in current annual revenues of USD 185.2 billion.

 

  • Founded: 1926
  • Daimler AG Headquarters: Mercedesstraße, Stuttgart, Germany
  • Daimler AG Revenue: USD 115.2 billion

 

6.    General Motors

 

6th on BizVibe’s list of the top 10 largest manufacturing companies in the world is the American multinational corporation headquartered in Detroit, General Motors. 6th among the leading manufacturing companies by revenue and the company was also ranked 13th in the USA by revenue for all companies by Fortune 500 rankings. General Motors brings in annual revenues of USD 137.2 billion.

 

  • Founded: 1908
  • General Motors Headquarters: Detroit, Michigan, US
  • General Motors Revenue: USD 137.2 billion
  • General Motors Number of Employees: 164,000

 

7.    Ford Motor Company

 

Ford Motor Company is an American multinational automobile manufacturer that has its main headquarters in Dearborn, Michigan. The company was founded in 1903 and brings in annual revenues of USD 155.9 billion — making it one the 10 largest manufacturing companies in the world.

 

  • Founded: 1903
  • Ford Motor Company Headquarters: Dearborn, Michigan
  • Ford Motor Company Revenue: USD 155.9 billion

 

8.    Hon Hai Precision Industry

 

8th on this list is Taiwanese multinational electronics contract manufacturer, Hon Hai Precision Industry, also known as Foxconn.  This company’s headquarters are in Tucheng District, New Taipei and is known for manufacturing electronic components, PCBs, PCB components, computer chips for its clients from all over the world.

Hon Hai Precision Industry was founded in 1974 and brings in annual revenues of USD 154.7 billion.

 

  • Founded: 1974
  • Hon Hai Precision Industry Headquarters: Tucheng District, New Taipei, Taiwan
  • Hon Hai Precision Industry Revenue: USD 154.7 billion

 

9.    Honda

 

Honda Motor Company is 9th on this list of largest manufacturing companies by revenue. Honda is a Japanese public multinational conglomerate corporation primarily known as a manufacturer of automobiles, motorcycles, and power equipment. The company was founded in             Hamamatsu, Japan in 1946. Honda’s other manufacturing divisions include Acura, Honda Aircraft Company, Honda Automobiles, and Honda Motorcycles.

 

  • Founded: 1946
  • Honda Headquarters: Minato, Tokyo, Japan
  • Honda Revenue: USD 138.7 billion
  • Honda Number of Employees: 215,638

 

10.  Cardinal Health

 

Rounding out the list is the first pharmaceutical company, Cardinal Health — an American multinational health care services company. The company specializes in manufacturing medical and pharmaceutical products and brought in USD 136.8 billion in revenue last year. Cardinal Health’s current headquarters are in Dublin, Ohio, U.S.

 

  • Founded: 1971
  • Cardinal Health Headquarters: Dublin, Ohio, U.S.
  • Cardinal Health Revenue: USD 136.8 billion
